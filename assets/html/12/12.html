About Us

The Pokémon Master Trade Fest is an annual event designed to bring trainers together. Whether you're looking to complete your Pokédex or meet new friends, this event is for you! Organized by the Pokémon Trainer Club, it fosters community connections through the joy of trading Pokémon.

Event highlights

  • Trading Zones: Special areas for beginners and advanced trainers.
  • Rare Pokémon Giveaway: Participate to win exclusive Pokémon trades.
  • Trading Competitions: Show off your trading skills and win exciting prizes.
